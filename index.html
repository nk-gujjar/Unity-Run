About Us

Unity Run is a flagship event by the Outdoor Adventure Club (ODAC) of IIT Ropar that celebrates the values of unity, endurance, and well-being.

Bringing together students, faculty, and the community, the run symbolizes the spirit of inclusiveness and fitness. ODAC, known for organizing treks, marathons, and adventure events, proudly leads this initiative every year.

Our mission is to create an environment where people from all backgrounds can come together to celebrate health, challenge themselves, and build lasting connections through the shared experience of running.
